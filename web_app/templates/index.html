RedPointer

RedPointer provides recommendations for the your next climbing project fitted to your personal style.

Find Out More

What it does?

RedPointer parses ratings from MountainProject route user ratings and return the climbing route you should do based on prior preferences. New to climbing? We can provide you hike recommendations based on your own preferences! Or completely at random! You choose.
